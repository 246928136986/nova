useful tools

use these tools to help you out with school work or anything else you want. all of them are free and don't need any sign-up!

general-use tools

chatgpt - great for research, brainstorming, etc. not much else to say about it, its literally just chatgpt lol

scira.app - input a topic, and it will give you a summary, images, etc. good for quick research

blooket hacks - coming soon!


math tools

mathway - suuuuuuuper good for math. just put in an equation and select what to do (simplify, solve, factor, etc).

wolfram alpha - not as good as mathway, but can still do most equations


image tools

ai image generator - generates an image of whatever you want

ai image enhancer - if you have pixely or blurry images, you can use this to make them look better and sharper

ai magic eraser - use this to erase unwanted objects, watermarks, etc from images

background remover - gets rid of the background on images and makes them transparent


bookmarklet utilities

inspect element - generates an image of whatever you want

website editor - if you have pixely or blurry images, you can use this to make them look better and sharper

html extractor - use this to erase unwanted objects, watermarks, etc from images

javascript executor - gets rid of the background on images and makes them transparent
